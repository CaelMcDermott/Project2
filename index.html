About our company

We sell games created by small developers so they can get their studio off the ground. With every sale, 75% of the money goes to the developers, 15% goes into protecting the enviroment, while just 10% goes to us.

who we are

OakTree Gaming was created and founded by a group of passionate gamers who wanted to help small game studios succeed, while at the same time improving their enviroment. The games on our website are created by zealous developers who want to impact the gaming indrustry with their games.
